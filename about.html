From idea to impact

2019
Zero-waste experiment in a Bondi studio. First 100 hand-filled bottles sold out in two weeks.
2021
Refill program launched; replaceable metal pumps extended product lifecycle.
2023
Partnerships with local growers for native extracts; community beach cleanups across NSW.
2025
Expanded to a multi-category eco store: skincare, bulk foods, and home goods.
